# Zebrão: The Kingdom of Quantity

A strange and metaphysical 2.5D/3D hybrid sandbox built with Three.js and Phaser 3. It acts as a metalinguistic critique of modern corporate trends that value a massive quantity of content over the quality of the individual experience.

The game features dynamic day and night cycles, procedural fog, and immersive polygonal textures. It invites the player to escape uniformity in an open, experimental, and purely strange work.
