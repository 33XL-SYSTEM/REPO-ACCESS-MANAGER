# SECURE FLAG // Forensic Cybersecurity Didactic Environment

**Secure Flag** is a Capture The Flag (CTF) educational ecosystem designed to bring university students closer to the practical world of cybersecurity and computer forensics. Originally developed for UNICEPLAC, the project was born to combat the shortage of trained professionals in digital security, offering a safe simulated environment for training investigative skills.

The simulation experience consists of two fictitious integrated web applications and a central challenge manager. The narrative revolves around a digital investigation of an alleged cyber attack on a fictitious financial group. Participants assume the role of forensic experts who must explore codes, decrypt files, analyze logs, and reverse binaries to discover hidden clues called "flags".

## Key Components

- **Yaldabaoth-Home:** A clean institutional portal serving as a facade to hide clues and social engineering anchors.
- **Yaldabaoth-Bank-Corporate:** A React 18/Vite application reproducing a corporate banking email system where complex cryptography and data analysis challenges reside.
- **Secure Flag CTF:** A Docker and CTFd-based platform responsible for automatic key validation, real-time ranking, and progressive scoring.
