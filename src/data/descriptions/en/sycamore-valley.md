# SYCAMORE VALLEY GAME // Horror and Paranormal Exploration

**Sycamore Valley** is a 2D horror and exploration game developed under a dense retro pixel-art aesthetic. The player takes the role of Special Agent Evan Brecht from the Paranormal Investigations Agency (AIP), tasked with investigating the mysterious disappearance of civilians and confronting a hostile entity known as Yaldabaoth.

The gameplay combines the classic charm of top-down adventure games with modern dynamic combat mechanics, managed by a Stamina and Focus System. In addition to physical combat, it relies heavily on investigative puzzles and collecting documentary clues.

Technically, the graphics engine operates natively and optimized on the web, utilizing the Web Audio API for spatialized audio effects that build sonic tension.
